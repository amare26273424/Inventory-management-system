1.1 Getting Started

Introduction to the system and its purpose for Staff Member

Welcome to the AMU ICT Store user help guide for staff members. This guide aims to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of the system's purpose and functionality to help you effectively navigate and utilize its features.

The AMU ICT Store is designed to streamline the process of managing product requests, approvals, and viewing relevant information for staff members like yourself. It serves as a centralized platform for requesting products, tracking the status of your requests, viewing product details, and accessing your previous records.

Purpose of the System:

The primary purpose of the AMU ICT Store is to facilitate a smooth and efficient workflow for staff members when it comes to requesting products and managing related processes. With this system, you can easily initiate product requests, view the approval or decline status of your requests, and access information about available products and your previous records.

NAVIGATION: Land on the login page of the system Enter a valid username and password Click on Login button and you will land on Staff Member Homepage Overview of user interface elements relevant to Staff.

Get Ready:
to observe the Staff Member Page, follow these steps:

Once logged in, you will land on the Store Management Staff Dashboard.

Filling the Form:
  1. On the dashboard or navigation bar, find the option to fill a form.
  2. Click on the form option to access the form submission page.
  3. Fill in the necessary details as required by the form.
  4. Review the form for accuracy and completeness.
  5. Submit the form to complete the process.
Viewing Products:
  1. On the navigation bar, locate the option to view products.
  2. Click on the product option to access the product listing page.
  3. Browse through the available products.
Viewing Transactions:
  1. On the navigation bar, find the option to view transactions.
  2. Click on the transaction option to access the transaction history page.
  3. Review the list of transactions and their details.

1.2 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for Staff Members

Q1: How do I access the Store Management System as a staff member?

A: To access the Store Management System, go to the login page and enter your valid username and password. Click on the Login button to proceed to the system.

Q2: How can request a product?

A: To request a product, navigate to the request form within the system.double click the product and the form product name will fill and Fill in the required details such as the, quantity, and any additional notes or specifications. Submit the form to initiate the product request.

Q3: How can I track the status of my product request?

A: You can track the status of your product request within the system. Check the relevant section or page where your requests are listed. The system will provide updates on the approval or decline status of your request.

Q4: Can I view the products available in the store?

A: Yes, you can view the products available in the store. Use the navigation menu to access the product listing page. Browse through the available products and click on a specific product to view its details such as description, price, and stock availability.

Q5: How can I view my previous records or transaction history?

A: To view your previous records or transaction history, navigate to the transaction history page or a similar section within the system. You will find a list of your past transactions along with relevant details.
